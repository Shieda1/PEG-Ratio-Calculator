What is the PEG ratio?
the price earnings to growth ratio (PEG ratio) is a stock's price earnings (P / E) ratio divided by the growth rate of it's earnings for a specific time period.
How to calculate PEG ratio?
To calculate PEG ratio you should use the following formula:
PEG ratio = P/E ratio / earnings growth rate

Why use PEG ratio?
the PEG ratio is important because it is used to determine a stock's value while factoring in the company's expected earnings growth
